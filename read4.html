Game Industry

The game industry is a major force within the entertainment industry, focusing on the creation, distribution, and monetization of video games. It's a massive and complex sector with surprising financial clout. Here's a closer look:

Game Development

Studios create video games for various platforms like consoles, PCs, and mobile devices. This involves designers, programmers, artists, writers, and many more specialists.

Game Publishing

Companies finance, market, and distribute video games, often working with developers to bring their titles to market.

Esports

Competitive gaming has become a professional sport, with organized tournaments, pro gamers, and spectator audiences. Esports even have broadcasting and streaming aspects.
