Music Production

Music production in the entertainment industry is the creative and technical process of bringing musical ideas to life for film, television, video games, and other forms of entertainment. It involves a wide range of roles and skills, working together to achieve a specific artistic vision and technical execution.

Composer

Write original music specifically for a film, television show, video game, or other media.

Song Writers

Create the lyrics and melodies for songs used in entertainment projects.

Music Editor

Assemble and edit recorded music to fit the specific needs of the project's timing and narrative flow.
