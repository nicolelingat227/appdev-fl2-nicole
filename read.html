Publishing

Publishing in the entertainment industry refers to the process of making creative content available to the public through various mediums such as books, magazine and comics. It involves the dissemination and distribution of intellectual property to reach audiences and generate revenue. Publishers in the entertainment industry can include traditional publishing houses, record labels, film studios, streaming platforms, and digital distributors, each specializing in different types of content and formats. The goal of publishing in entertainment is to connect creators with audiences while managing the commercial aspects of intellectual property rights and distribution channels.
