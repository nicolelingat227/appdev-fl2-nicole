Our Services

At General Industry, we offer a wide range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients in the entertainment industry. Our commitment to excellence ensures that each service is delivered with the highest quality and professionalism.

We produce captivating films and TV shows that entertain and inspire audiences around the world. Our team handles everything from concept development to post-production.

Our music production services cover recording, mixing, and mastering. We work with talented artists to create hit songs and memorable soundtracks.

Digital Media

Our digital media services include web series, podcasts, and interactive content, designed to engage and entertain audiences across digital platforms.

Talent Management

We represent a diverse roster of talent, providing career management, branding, and promotional services to help artists achieve their full potential.

Content Distribution

Our content distribution network ensures that your media reaches a global audience through various channels, including streaming services and traditional media.
